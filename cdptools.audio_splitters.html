Bases: abc.ABC

Why is this not just a single function?

Making it available to the backend maintainers to pass arguments to the instance of the class and retain state may be useful. An example that I can think of: Instead of splitting the entire video into a single audio clip, a parameter could be passed to the instance that splits the audio into smaller portions and needs to track additional metadata.
